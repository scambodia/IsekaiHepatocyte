📖 Description

A dark-comedy biological survival game where you are reincarnated as a single liver cell (Hepatocyte) inside a host with an absolutely terrible lifestyle. You can't move, you forgot all your biochemistry, but you must survive! Manage your ATP (Energy), fight off the host's toxic habits (alcohol, fatty foods), and eventually mutate your way out of the dying liver.

If you manage to escape... Stage 2 begins. Survive as a metastatic cancer cell balancing immune evasion, resource theft, and keeping your host alive just long enough to achieve immortality.

✨ Features

Two Distinct Stages: * Stage 1 (Survival): Manage toxicity and ATP to prevent apoptosis (cell death) and achieve metastasis.

Stage 2 (Malignancy): Balance immune threat and host health while proliferating to reach 100% cancer spread.

Dynamic Event Log: Hilarious, Thai-flavored random events based on the host's chaotic lifestyle (e.g., eating raw meat at midnight, drinking heavily, or a miracle vegetable meal).

Visual Evolution: The cell's appearance and the environment dynamically change based on toxicity levels and current game stage.

Difficulty Settings: Adjust incoming damage multipliers and passive ATP regeneration to suit your playstyle.

TenSura-inspired Flavor: Features helpful (and sometimes sarcastic) "Great Sage" style notifications.

🎮 How to Play

Monitor your Resources: Keep an eye on your ATP (Energy). It regenerates passively, but you need it to perform crucial survival actions.

Survive the Host: Manage your Toxicity (Stage 1) or Immune Threat (Stage 2). If these bars hit 100, your cell dies and the game is over.

Take Action: Click the action buttons to metabolize fat, process alcohol, build defensive shields, or force mutations.

Win Condition: Fill your Mutation / Cancer Spread bar to 100% to unlock your Ultimate skill and advance to the next phase or achieve the ultimate ending!

🛠️ Tech Stack

Frontend: HTML5, CSS3 (Custom properties, Flexbox/Grid layouts, CSS Animations)

Logic: Vanilla JavaScript (ES6)

Architecture: Fully static, single-file application. No backend or database required.

🚀 Deployment (GitHub Pages)

This game is designed to be easily hosted on GitHub Pages:

Upload index.html to the root of your repository.

Go to your repository Settings -> Pages.

Under "Build and deployment", set the source to Deploy from a branch.

Select the main (or master) branch and save.

GitHub will generate a live URL for your game within a few minutes!
